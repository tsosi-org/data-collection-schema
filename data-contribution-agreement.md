# TSOSI Data Contribution Agreement

## Introduction

TSOSI is a web platform launched in June 2025 by Université Grenoble Alpes. The web platform TSOSI (tsosi.org) highlights support provided to open science infrastructures. TSOSI data can come from many sources, such as research institutions, consortia, research funders, ministries and government bodies, or directly from open infrastructures. TSOSI publishes data from the web platform and via its open API.

## Parties

- Université Grenoble Alpes is a French public research institution located in Grenoble, France. Its ROR identifier is https://ror.org/02rx3b187.

- The TSOSI team comprises the persons who operate TSOSI (tsosi.org)

- The organisation that shares data via the TSOSI platform is called a _Provider_.

## Terms

To include Provider data on the TSOSI platform, you agree to the following terms:

- You confirm you have authority to bind the Provider.
- The Provider is solely responsible for the accuracy of the data and must inform the TSOSI team of any corrections.
- The TSOSI team and the Provider agree to update the data at least once a year.
- You agree that Université Grenoble Alpes will publish the data you provided on the TSOSI platform (tsosi.org).
- The data you share will be published under the terms of the [Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/).
- Your personal information will not be published and will be used in accordance with the [TSOSI Privacy Policy](https://tsosi.org/pages/privacy-policy).
- The collected data do not confer intellectual property rights; this agreement does not transfer intellectual property rights.
- This data-sharing agreement does not involve any financial exchange.
- If the TSOSI project ends, the Provider will be notified by email and the platform will remain online for one year from the date of notification.


## Changes

TSOSI may update these terms or add conditions for contributing to TSOSI; TSOSI will post changes on the TSOSI site, and your continued use after notice constitutes acceptance.
